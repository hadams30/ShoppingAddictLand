There are some important questions you can ask yourself before beginning shopping:

  1. Is there something that's triggering me to shop? Stress, boredom, or anxiety can cause your mind to search out ways to gain some quick happiness. If you're feeling any of these, you may want to take a few breaths and engage in a different activity.
  2. Do I really need this right now? A common tip is to leave the item for 24 hours and if you've forgotten about it, then you probably don't need it - You can also add items to a wishlist instead!
  3. How many emails or texts have I recently received about a sale or new item? Companies love to send you promotions or notices about products going on sale. It's a trap! Consider unsubscribing from their communications to keep it off your mind.
  4. Have I set any limits for myself? It can be easy to spend and purchase when you aren't holding yourself accountable, but those things add up! Further down this page, we talk about setting up a budget that helps you create boundaries to how much you can and have to spend.
